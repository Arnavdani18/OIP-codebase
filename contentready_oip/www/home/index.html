Mission to create social impact

The Open Innovation Exchange is a problem-centric platform to systematically manage the lab to market journey of innovative solutions that solve a set of validated problem statements. This platform will bring all possible stakeholders needed in this process right from the end beneficiary at the grassroot level, innovators, entrepreneurs, academicians & researchers, volunteers, development organizations/NGOs, philanthropic foundations, corporate CSR, incubators and other ecosystem players.
